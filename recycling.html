Why Recycle E-Waste?

  • Reduces landfill waste.
  • Protects the environment.
  • Recovers valuable materials.
  • Prevents pollution.
  • Supports sustainability.

Benefits of Recycling

Recycling electronic products helps recover valuable metals and reduces the need for mining new resources. It also prevents harmful chemicals from entering the environment.

How Does E-Waste Recycling Work?

  1. Old electronic devices are collected.
  2. They are sorted into different categories.
  3. Reusable devices may be repaired and refurbished.
  4. Materials such as plastics, glass and metals are separated.
  5. Recovered materials are used to manufacture new products.

What Can You Do?

  • Donate working electronics.
  • Repair devices instead of replacing them.
  • Recycle unwanted electronics responsibly.
  • Delete personal data before recycling.
  • Buy durable products that last longer.
