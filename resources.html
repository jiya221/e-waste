Tips for Safe Disposal

  • Remove batteries before recycling if required.
  • Back up important files.
  • Factory reset phones and computers.
  • Delete personal information.
  • Take devices to certified recycling centres.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can broken electronics be recycled?

Yes. Even broken devices contain valuable materials that can be recovered.

Can batteries go into household rubbish?

No. Batteries contain hazardous chemicals and should be recycled at designated collection points.

Why should I erase my data?

Phones and computers may contain personal information. Erasing your data helps protect your privacy.
